Invest in Us

Why should you partner with us?

SRIT’s primary focus is on transforming industries such as healthcare, governance and telecom. Instead of focusing on digital automation of micro-processes or niche functions, we believe that it is far more effective to target large important nodes such as healthcare and government, and service and automate them from start to finish, such that their benefits ripple out to a far greater number of people in a much shorter span of time, thereby transforming an entire city, an entire state, and even an entire country in leaps and bounds.

In order to achieve these visions, we partner with a number of reputed names in the field, believing that such collaborations allow us to leverage each other’s strengths, minimize weaknesses, and arrive at a better solution faster. We wholeheartedly welcome organizations, individuals and bureaucracies to come and partner with us on various projects in the fields of Healthcare, Governance and Telecom.
